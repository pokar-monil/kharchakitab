## Typing karna kaun chahta hai?

Auto se utre, 80 rupees diye — ab phone nikaalke app kholo, amount type karo, category dhundo, save karo. Tab tak agle auto mein baith gaye.

Yahi problem hai. Kharcha yaad rehta hai, time nahi rehta.

## Sirf bolo — KharchaKitab baaki sambhal leta hai

Mic tap karo, bolo:

> "auto 80 rupees"

App khud nikal leta hai:
- Amount: ₹80
- Category: Transport
- Date: aaj

Koi form nahi, koi dropdown nahi.

## Hinglish mein bolo — sab chalega

- "chai 20 rupees"
- "Swiggy se pizza mangaya 450"
- "rent 15000 de diya landlord ko"
- "metro card recharge 500"

Hindi, English, mix — jo natural lage.

## Kaise kaam karta hai

1. Mic tap karo
2. Kharcha bolo — naturally, jaise kisi dost ko bata rahe ho
3. AI amount, category, date parse karta hai
4. Entry save

3 second. Bas.

## Kab use karein

Jab bhi paisa jaaye — auto, chai, grocery, bill — turant bolo. Baad mein yaad nahi rehta.
